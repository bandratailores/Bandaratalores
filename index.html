Measurement Guide

1

Bust Measurement

Measure around the fullest part of your bust while wearing a bra. Keep the tape parallel to the ground.

2

Waist Measurement

Measure around the narrowest part of your waist, usually just above the belly button.

3

Shoulder Width

Measure from one shoulder seam to the other across your back.

4

Sleeve Length

Measure from shoulder seam to desired sleeve length with arm slightly bent.
